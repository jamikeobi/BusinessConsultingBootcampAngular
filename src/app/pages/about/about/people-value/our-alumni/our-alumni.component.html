Welcome to KWAMU Professional Services Nigeria Alumni Network!

We take immense pride in the accomplishments of our former team members and are dedicated to fostering lifelong connections. At KWAMU Nigeria, we view our ex-colleagues as integral members of our global family and maintain strong ties with them through various channels, including specially curated alumni events.

Our alumni program serves as a platform for rekindling friendships, accessing industry insights, training opportunities, and staying updated on the latest developments within our firm and across the broader alumni community.

Joining the Alumni Programme is both free and rewarding. By subscribing, you'll enjoy the following benefits:

Industry Insights: Access KWAMU thought leadership, surveys, and reports. Alumni News: Receive our biannual newsletter featuring alumni spotlights. Events: Access KWAMU thought leadership, surveys, and reports. Discounts: Avail exclusive discounts on a range of products and services. LinkedIn Group: Connect with felloe alumni via the KWAMU Nigeria LinkedIn Group. Job Opportunities: Gain Access to job openings within and outside the firm.

We invite you to become a part of our alumni network to seize these benefits and stay connected with fellow professionals, events, and valuable resources. We eagerly anticipate staying in touch with you.
