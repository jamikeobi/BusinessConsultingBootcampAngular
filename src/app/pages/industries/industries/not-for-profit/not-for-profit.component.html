Not for profit

Operating in the not-for-profit sector presents many challenges. Change is constant. So too is the pressure to raise funds, the impact of government policy, changing demographics and expectations to deliver more on a daily basis.We understand the commitment and scrutiny within this sector and will work with you to meet these challenges.

We understand that ‘not-for-profit’ describes a number of different types of organisations, including educational institutions, charities, healthcare, sporting and community clubs, and membership organisations. With this in mind, we provide advice that reflects the nature of your organisation — with your unique needs, challenges and opportunities.

By drawing on the experience of our advisers in core service lines, we can provide solutions for not-for-profit companies that tackle key areas of concern and ultimately allow you to become proactive in managing issues. We work with you to understand the factors that drive your organization and how to utilize its effectiveness.
