International Financial Reporting Standards

IFRS is the common global financial reporting language. Major new standards for financial instruments, revenue recognition and leasing present significant challenges to preparers of financial statements.

BDO’s IFRS compliance requires efficient systems, global reach, and hands-on advisory. BDO’s dedicated professionals provide standardized reporting services, as well as the industry’s highest levels of insight and technical knowledge.

BDO’s professionals possess cross-cultural understanding and experience, allowing us unique insight into the people, languages, and accounting practices of clients in all geographical areas of the country.
