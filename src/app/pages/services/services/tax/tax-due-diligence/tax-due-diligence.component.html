Tax Due Diligence

We provide tax due diligence services for acquisitions, merger, and disposals of companies. Our services checks that the target company is all that it claims to be and ensures that the purchaser does not acquire unknown liabilities.

BDO Professional Services has a wealth of experience in tax due diligence reviews, which involve identifying the tax exposure on transactions and how it can be mitigated.

Tax Diligence services include

  • Reviews client tax profile and filing history to assess potential risk and opportunities
  • Preparation of a taxation due diligence report covering the corporate tax, PAYE and VAT position of the company and highlighting areas of risk and potential liabilities in order for these to be addressed as part of the transaction process
  • Post acquisition tax advisory
